## Free Alternatives to Calm

Calm is popular for its Sleep Stories and relaxation content, but the $69.99/year subscription puts most of it behind a paywall. The free tier gives you one Daily Calm session per day and a few nature sounds — that's about it. If you want meditation and sleep content without ongoing costs, free alternatives deliver.

## What Calm Charges For

Calm's subscription unlocks Sleep Stories (celebrity-narrated bedtime stories), its full meditation library, masterclasses, and focus music. The free version is essentially a sample. The app requires account creation, and the free trial needs a credit card. Calm has a Trustpilot score of about 1.6/5, largely due to cancellation and billing frustrations.
