## Free Alternatives to Simple Habit

Simple Habit built its reputation on short, 5-minute meditation sessions designed for busy schedules. It's a practical concept — meditation that fits into a lunch break or commute. But at $95.99/year for full access, the subscription cost is higher than most competitors. If you want quick guided meditations without the price tag, free alternatives exist.

## What Simple Habit Charges For

Simple Habit's free tier gives you a limited selection of sessions. The full library — including meditations for specific situations like commuting, work breaks, and difficult conversations — requires the premium subscription. The app needs account creation and uses a free trial model to convert users to paid plans.
