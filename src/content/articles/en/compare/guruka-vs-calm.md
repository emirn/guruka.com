## What Calm Does Well

Calm's Sleep Stories are genuinely unique. Having well-known voices narrate bedtime stories designed to help you fall asleep is a creative idea, and it works. The production quality is high, and there's nothing quite like it on any other platform.

The Daily Calm feature — a fresh 10-minute guided meditation each day — gives users a reason to come back. It's a smart engagement mechanism that also serves as a solid daily practice anchor. The app's visual design is polished, with nature scenes and ambient sounds that create a relaxing atmosphere from the moment you open it.

Calm has also expanded into workplace wellness and mental health content beyond meditation, including masterclasses on topics like gratitude and mindful eating. If you're looking for a broad wellness platform rather than just meditation, Calm covers more ground.

## What Calm Charges For

Calm's free tier is one of the most restrictive among meditation apps. You get one Daily Calm session per day and a handful of nature sounds. That's essentially the entire free offering — Sleep Stories, the full meditation library, focus music, masterclasses, and everything else requires the $69.99/year subscription.

The free trial requires a credit card and auto-renews. Calm has a Trustpilot score of approximately 1.6 out of 5, with the majority of negative reviews citing unexpected charges after the trial period and difficulty canceling subscriptions.
