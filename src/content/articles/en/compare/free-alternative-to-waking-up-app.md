## Free Alternatives to the Waking Up App

Waking Up, created by neuroscientist and author Sam Harris, is a meditation app that goes beyond relaxation into the nature of consciousness itself. It takes a philosophical, non-religious approach that appeals to intellectually curious meditators. The app costs $99.99/year, though Harris offers free subscriptions to anyone who can't afford it. If you want guided meditation without a subscription, free alternatives are available.

## What Makes Waking Up Different

Waking Up focuses on what Harris calls "non-dual awareness" — the idea that the sense of being a separate self is an illusion that meditation can dissolve. The app includes a 28-day introductory course, daily meditations, "Theory" lessons on philosophy and consciousness, and conversations with other teachers. It's more intellectually rigorous than most meditation apps, which is its appeal — and its limitation for people who just want to relax.
