## Free Alternatives to the Balance App

Balance is a meditation app known for its personalized meditation plans that adapt to your experience level. It gained popularity by offering a free first year, but once that trial ends, it costs $69.99/year. If you're looking for meditation without ongoing subscription fees, several free alternatives are available.

## What Balance Offers

Balance creates personalized daily meditation sessions based on your answers to check-in questions. It covers focus, sleep, stress reduction, and other categories. The app is well-designed and the personalization is a genuine differentiator. However, after the free year expires, the full subscription kicks in — and many users discover they need to keep paying to access their meditation habit.
