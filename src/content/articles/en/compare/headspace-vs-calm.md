## Headspace Strengths

Headspace excels at structured learning. Its Basics course walks beginners through meditation fundamentals over 30 days, starting with 3-minute sessions. The content is professionally produced, and co-founder Andy Puddicombe's teaching style is clear and approachable. If you want a curriculum-based approach and don't mind the subscription, Headspace delivers.

## Calm Strengths

Calm is best known for its Sleep Stories — narrated bedtime stories by celebrities like Matthew McConaughey and Harry Styles. It also offers a broader range of ambient music and nature soundscapes. If sleep content is your primary reason for using a meditation app, Calm has the largest library.

## The Paywall Problem

Both Headspace and Calm lock most of their content behind a $69.99/year subscription. Their free tiers are essentially demos. Headspace gives you about 10 introductory sessions. Calm gives you one Daily Calm per day and a few nature sounds. After a week or two, you'll hit a wall.

Both apps also use free trial models that require a credit card upfront, and cancellation can be frustrating. Headspace has a Trustpilot score of ~1.8/5, and Calm sits at ~1.6/5, largely due to billing complaints.
