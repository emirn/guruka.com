## Free Alternatives to Smiling Mind

Smiling Mind is a free, nonprofit meditation app developed in Australia with backing from the Australian government. It's well-made and genuinely free — no subscriptions, no ads. But it was originally designed for schools and young people, and adult users often find the content limited. If you want a free meditation tool with more depth for adults, alternatives exist.

## Where Smiling Mind Falls Short for Adults

Smiling Mind offers about 300 sessions organized into programs for different age groups and life stages. The quality is good and the app is completely free. However, the content skews toward younger audiences, the library is relatively small compared to other options, and the app requires account creation. Some adult users report outgrowing the available content within a few months.
